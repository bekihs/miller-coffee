Adam's story

The Coffee Miller was founded by Adam Miller after his six-year travels and research in African and South American coffee farms.

Adam fell in love with the third-wave of coffee and has embraced the slow and natural way of making coffee.

We welcome you to taste the difference.

Barista training and mastering

We facilitate barista training courses and latte art workshops at different locations and businesses

Business

Would you like to have your service personnel to become top notch baristas and coffee makers? Our enthusiastic trainers will provide your staff with the best step by step instruction starting with coffee growing, harvesting & roasting, moving to setting up a coffee grinder, an espresso coffee machine and steaming milk to perfection. All a barista needs to know in one course.

Private

For people who loves coffee and want to take their home - made coffee to the next level. In this course you will learn the hi story and art behind cappuccino, flat white, caffe latte and of course, practice the Latte Art of hearts, wheat and animal shapes to keep it extra fun! Be prepared to drink and taste lots and lots of coffee!
